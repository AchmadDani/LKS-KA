A. Studi Kasus A (Categorical)

  • Prior: \(P(Yes) = 9/14\), \(P(No) = 5/14\)
  • Zero Likelihood: \(P(Hot \mid Yes) = 0/9 = 0\), \(P(Overcast \mid No) = 0/5 = 0\)
  • Laplace Outlook Yes: Sunny = 3/12, Overcast = 5/12, Rain = 4/12
  • Laplace Outlook No: Sunny = 4/8, Overcast = 1/8, Rain = 3/8
  • Laplace Temperature Yes: Hot = 1/12, Mild = 7/12, Cool = 4/12
  • Laplace Temperature No: Hot = 3/8, Mild = 3/8, Cool = 2/8
  • Posterior Score Yes: \(\frac{5}{12} \times \frac{1}{12} \times \frac{4}{11} \times \frac{4}{11} \times \frac{9}{14} \approx \mathbf{0.00376}\)
  • Posterior Score No: \(\frac{1}{8} \times \frac{3}{8} \times \frac{5}{7} \times \frac{4}{7} \times \frac{5}{14} \approx \mathbf{0.00854}\)
  • Keputusan Akhir: No (Tidak Bermain)

B. Studi Kasus B (Gaussian)

  • \(P(Battery=28 \mid No) \approx \mathbf{0.0000021}\)
  • \(P(Camera=4 \mid No) \approx \mathbf{0.2173}\)
  • \(P(Price=2 \mid No) \approx \mathbf{0.0101}\)
  • \(P(Battery=28 \mid Yes) \approx \mathbf{0.0116}\)
  • \(P(Camera=4 \mid Yes) \approx \mathbf{0.0039}\)
  • \(P(Price=2 \mid Yes) \approx \mathbf{0.0754}\)
  • Posterior No: \(2.11 \times 10^{-6} \times 0.2173 \times 0.0101 \times (6/14) \approx \mathbf{1.98 \times 10^{-9}}\)
  • Posterior Yes: \(0.0116 \times 0.0039 \times 0.0754 \times (8/14) \approx \mathbf{1.95 \times 10^{-6}}\)
  • Keputusan Akhir: Yes (Direkomendasikan)
