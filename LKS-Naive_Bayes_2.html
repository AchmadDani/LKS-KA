Kunci Jawaban Lengkap & Pembahasan Soal

Bagian 1 & 2: Play Tennis

  • Prior: \(P(Yes) = 9/14 \approx \mathbf{0.6429}\), \(P(No) = 5/14 \approx \mathbf{0.3571}\)
  • Outlook Likelihood:
    • Sunny | Yes = 2/9 (\(0.2222\)), Sunny | No = 3/5 (\(0.6000\))
    • Overcast | Yes = 4/9 (\(0.4444\)), Overcast | No = 0/5 (\(0.0000\))
    • Rain | Yes = 3/9 (\(0.3333\)), Rain | No = 2/5 (\(0.4000\))
  • Prediksi Manual \(X = (Sunny, Cool, Normal, Strong)\):
    • \(P(Yes|X) \propto 9/14 \times 2/9 \times 3/9 \times 6/9 \times 3/9 = 2/189 \approx \mathbf{0.0106}\)
    • \(P(No|X) \propto 5/14 \times 3/5 \times 1/5 \times 1/5 \times 3/5 = 9/1750 \approx \mathbf{0.0051}\)
    • Keputusan: \(0.0106 > 0.0051 \rightarrow \mathbf{Yes}\)

Bagian 3: Laplace Smoothing

  • P(Hot | No) dengan Smoothing: \(\frac{0+1}{5+3} = 1/8 = \mathbf{0.1250}\)
  • Prediksi \(X = (Overcast, Hot, Normal, Weak)\):
    • \(P(Yes|X) \propto 9/14 \times \frac{4+1}{9+3} \times \frac{2+1}{9+3} \times \frac{6+1}{9+2} \times \frac{6+1}{9+2} = \frac{6615}{243936} \approx \mathbf{0.0271}\)
    • \(P(No|X) \propto 5/14 \times \frac{0+1}{5+3} \times \frac{2+1}{5+3} \times \frac{1+1}{5+2} \times \frac{2+1}{5+2} = \frac{90}{43904} \approx \mathbf{0.0021}\)
    • Keputusan: \(0.0271 > 0.0021 \rightarrow \mathbf{Yes}\)

Bagian 4: Smartphone Recommendation

  • Prior: \(P(Yes) = 9/15 = \mathbf{0.6000}\), \(P(No) = 6/15 = \mathbf{0.4000}\)
  • Conditional Probabilities (Laplace Smoothing):
    • Battery High: Yes = 6/12 (\(0.5000\)), No = 1/9 (\(0.1111\))
    • RAM 4GB: Yes = 5/12 (\(0.4167\)), No = 2/9 (\(0.2222\))
    • Storage 64GB: Yes = 4/12 (\(0.3333\)), No = 3/9 (\(0.3333\))
    • Camera 8MP: Yes = 1/12 (\(0.0833\)), No = 6/9 (\(0.6667\))
    • Price Expensive: Yes = 5/12 (\(0.4167\)), No = 1/9 (\(0.1111\))
  • Prediksi Data Uji 1 \(X_1 = (Medium, 4GB, 64GB, 8MP, Medium)\):
    • \(P(Yes|X_1) \propto 0.6 \times \frac{5}{12} \times \frac{5}{12} \times \frac{4}{12} \times \frac{1}{12} \times \frac{6}{12} \approx \mathbf{0.0043}\)
    • \(P(No|X_1) \propto 0.4 \times \frac{2}{9} \times \frac{2}{9} \times \frac{3}{9} \times \frac{6}{9} \times \frac{1}{9} \approx \mathbf{0.0015}\)
    • Keputusan: \(0.0043 > 0.0015 \rightarrow \mathbf{Yes}\)
  • Prediksi Data Uji 2 \(X_2 = (Low, 4GB, 128GB, 8MP, Expensive)\):
    • \(P(Yes|X_2) \propto 0.6 \times \frac{1}{12} \times \frac{5}{12} \times \frac{7}{12} \times \frac{1}{12} \times \frac{5}{12} \approx \mathbf{0.0004}\)
    • \(P(No|X_2) \propto 0.4 \times \frac{6}{9} \times \frac{2}{9} \times \frac{1}{9} \times \frac{6}{9} \times \frac{1}{9} \approx \mathbf{0.0015}\)
    • Keputusan: \(0.0004 < 0.0015 \rightarrow \mathbf{No}\)
