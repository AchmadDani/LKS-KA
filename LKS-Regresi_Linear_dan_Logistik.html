Bagian 1: Regresi Linear Univariat

  • Rata-rata: \(\bar{x} = \frac{2+4+6+8+10}{5} = \mathbf{6.0}\), \(\bar{y} = \frac{27+29+35+52+59}{5} = \mathbf{40.4}\)
  • Varians X: \(\sum (x - \bar{x})^2 = (-4)^2 + (-2)^2 + 0^2 + 2^2 + 4^2 = \mathbf{40.0}\)
  • Kovarians X,Y: \(\sum (x - \bar{x})(y - \bar{y}) = (-4)(-13.4) + (-2)(-11.4) + (0)(-5.4) + (2)(11.6) + (4)(18.6) = \mathbf{174.0}\)
  • Parameter Bobot: \(w = \frac{174.0}{40.0} = \mathbf{4.35}\)
  • Parameter Bias: \(a = \bar{y} - (w \cdot \bar{x}) = 40.4 - (4.35 \times 6) = \mathbf{14.3}\)
  • Persamaan Garis: \(f(x) = 4.35x + 14.3\)
  • SSE untuk tabel Langkah 2 (Menggunakan Model w=4.35, a=14.3):
    $$x = 2 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 23.0 \rightarrow (51 - 23.0)^2 = 784.0$$ $$x = 4 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 31.7 \rightarrow (46 - 31.7)^2 = 204.49$$ $$x = 6 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 40.4 \rightarrow (60 - 40.4)^2 = 384.16$$ $$x = 8 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 49.1 \rightarrow (85 - 49.1)^2 = 1288.81$$ $$x = 10 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 57.8 \rightarrow (96 - 57.8)^2 = 1459.24$$ $$\mathbf{Total\ SSE = 4120.7}$$
  • SSE untuk tabel Langkah 2 (Menggunakan model optimal tersendiri w=6.45, a=28.9):
    $$x = 2 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 41.8 \rightarrow (51 - 41.8)^2 = 84.64$$ $$x = 4 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 54.7 \rightarrow (46 - 54.7)^2 = 75.69$$ $$x = 6 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 67.6 \rightarrow (60 - 67.6)^2 = 57.76$$ $$x = 8 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 80.5 \rightarrow (85 - 80.5)^2 = 20.25$$ $$x = 10 \rightarrow \hat{y} = 93.4 \rightarrow (96 - 93.4)^2 = 6.76$$ $$\mathbf{Total\ SSE = 245.1}$$

Bagian 2: Regresi Linear Multivariat

  • Matriks data input: $$X^TX = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 11 & 7 \\ 11 & 45 & 30 \\ 7 & 30 & 21 \end{bmatrix} \qquad (X^TX)^{-1} = \frac{1}{9} \begin{bmatrix} 45 & -21 & 15 \\ -21 & 14 & -13 \\ 15 & -13 & 14 \end{bmatrix}$$
  • Penyelesaian bobot \(w\): $$w = (X^TX)^{-1}X^Ty = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{-1.0} \\ \mathbf{2.67} \\ \mathbf{0.67} \end{bmatrix}$$
  • Persamaan Garis Multivariat: \(f(x) = -1.0 + 2.67x_1 + 0.67x_2\)
  • Prediksi Nilai Budi (\(x_1 = 5, x_2 = 2.5\)): $$f(5, 2.5) = -1.0 + 2.67(5) + 0.67(2.5) = -1.0 + 13.35 + 1.675 = 14.025 \approx \mathbf{14}$$

Bagian 3: Regresi Logistik

  • Parameter Optimal: \(w = 1.5, a = -9.0\)
  • Substitusi Jam Belajar Siti (\(x = 8\)): $$v = wx + a = 1.5(8) + (-9) = 12 - 9 = \mathbf{3.0}$$
  • Fungsi Aktivasi Sigmoid: $$\sigma(v) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-v}} = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-3}} = \frac{1}{1 + 0.0498} \approx \mathbf{0.9526} \text{ (95.26\%)}$$
  • Keputusan Akhir: Karena probabilitas \(0.9526 \ge 0.5\), Siti diprediksi Lulus.
